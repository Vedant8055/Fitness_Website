About Us

Our mission is to encourage all peoples to be the healthy and fit.

In a world where so much is being done for technology and so little for the environment, fitness is not even a part of most discussions.

We at VMuscles believe that by widening the reach of fitness, by making it freely available, so much can be achieved.

And this journey started in 2022 when a young boy thought "wouldn't it be great, to have a website, with simple tutorials for workouts, just like a friend would teach you!", and Vmuscles was born.
