Calves

Seated Calf Raise

Difficulty : beginner

.

    1. Use a seated calf raise machine or sit on a bench and rest the balls of your feet on a block or step (and hold dumbbells on your thighs for resistance).

    2. Allow your heels to drift toward the floor until you feel a stretch in your calves

    3. Now drive the balls of your feet into the platform and raise your heels as high as possible.



Standing Calf Raise

Difficulty : Beginner

.

    1. Use a standing calf raise machine or stand on a block or step with a dumbbell in one hand while holding on to something for support with the other.

    2. Lower your heels toward the floor until you feel a stretch in your calves.

    3. Drive the balls of your feet into the footplate and contract your calves, raising your heels as high as possible. Control the descent on each rep.
